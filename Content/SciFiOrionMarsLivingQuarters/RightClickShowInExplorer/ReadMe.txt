To enable the charater to move.

Import Input files to the project setting under "Inputs"

"Inputs" for third and first Preson

"Inputs_VR" for VR

To Interact with Elevator. Refridgerator, Frezzer, Closets and Drawers:

Place two pixel red dot over buttons or objects and press the "E" key.

Other Keys:

Mouse Wheel - First to Third Person

F - for Flashlight

CapLock - Toggles walk speed

Left Control - Hold to Crouch, Release to unCrouch

Left Shift - Hold to run

X - Toggles Slow Motion
